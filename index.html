Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Easy 6 Minute Recipe

Refreshing, Healthy, and Delicious

This smoothie is absolutely wonderous for when you need a quick, healthy, nutritious snack.

You only need a few ingredients and a blender. Thatâ€™s it!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup milk or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Add the banana and strawberries to the blender.
  2. Pour in the milk and optional honey.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
